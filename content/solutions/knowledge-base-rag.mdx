# Intelligent Knowledge Base AI

Convert your company documents, manuals, and resources into an intelligent knowledge base that provides instant, accurate answers using advanced RAG technology.

## Overview

Intelligent Knowledge Base AI uses Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) to search through your entire document library and deliver precise, contextual answers. Employees get the information they need in seconds, not hours.
